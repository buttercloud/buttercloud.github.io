Creating Rewards

A loyalty program is a powerful tool for businesses to retain customers and encourage repeat business. One of the key elements of a successful loyalty program is offering rewards that members will love. With Upzaar's loyalty platform, you can create custom rewards that are as unique as your business.

Creating Amount-Off or Discount-Based Rewards​

One of the most common types of rewards that businesses offer is discounts or amount-off rewards. These rewards give members a set discount on future purchases or services. With Upzaar's loyalty platform, you can easily create these types of rewards and configure the number of points required to redeem them.

Define Your Own Point Thresholds for Every Reward​

This means that you can set the number of points required to redeem a reward based on its value. For example, a more valuable reward may require a higher number of points to redeem, while a smaller reward may require fewer points.

Go Beyond Discounts and Credit​

While discounts and credit-based rewards are popular and effective, they may not be the best fit for every business. That's why Upzaar's loyalty platform allows you to offer experiences in exchange for points. These can be things like charity donations, travel experiences, or even custom merchandise. The possibilities are endless and limited only by your imagination.

Creating a Reward​

Creating a reward with Upzaar's loyalty platform is easy. All you need to do is enter the name, description, and point redemption value. The platform will handle the rest, including automatic point tracking and redemption.
